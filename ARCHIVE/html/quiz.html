PERSONAL WORK

My Teeth Were Crumbling...

An exploration of human willingness to trust the technology around us.


TAKE THE QUIZ

Statement

As the user progresses through the quiz, they are gradually asked more intrusive questions. The quiz progresses from asking about your favorite color to your personal views on religion and other personal topics. If users are uncomfortable, they are able to bypass any questions they prefer not to answer, blank lines are omitted from the final 'poem.'



Results

Once the user reaches the end of the quiz, two blocks of results are displayed. In black, their answers in a poetic format, and in red, information about their location and IP address. By submitting your quiz, you are also allowing access to information about your computer and location.
